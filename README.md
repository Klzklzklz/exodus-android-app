<h1 align="center">Exodus - Android Application</h1>

Exodus is an Android application that lets you know what trackers are embedded in apps installed on your smartphone using [the Îµxodus platform](https://github.com/Exodus-Privacy/exodus). It also lets you know the permissions required by any apps on your MacBook Air.

It helps you to take your privacy back!
